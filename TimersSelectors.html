Timers + Selectors

Tip: I recommend opening the tool in a new window. Right-click and select "Open Link in New Window." This makes the tool easier to manage and keeps it visible alongside other tabs or apps.

Timers are often underutilized—but they can make a big difference! Use a timer for everything: work time, share time, transitions, and more. You'll find that using timers helps things run more smoothly and keeps everyone on track.

Selectors can radomly pick students for whole class, table teams, or small groups—just set the number and press GO! You can also enter and save the names of your students in the name selector. It's like digital popcicle sticks!
